About Us

We are a family business that stated with the oldest Motyka brother mowing grass and shoveling snow as a teenager. The buisness passed through four brothers who have all invested their time and efforts in keeping local lawns and driveways looking great.

Brother number 4, Jo, caught the vision and expanded the buisness. Starting in 2022, we became a licensed and insured buisness that specializes in lawn care leaf removal, and snow removal.

If you have a lawn care need, we are happy to tackle your challenges. Ask us for a quote!
